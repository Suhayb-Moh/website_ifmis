What is IFMIS?

The Somaliland Financial Management Information System (SLFMIS) is a web based integrated suite of bespoke modules designed to automate the PFM cycle, from budget planning to budget execution, including payroll processing and collection of both tax and non-tax revenue. The SLFMIS enables accountability and transparency through audit trails and segregation of duties, and facilitates reporting in compliance with International Public Sector Accounting Standards (IPSAS).

IFMIS Funcationality

The SLFMIS is enabling the full automation policy position adopted by the Ministry of Finance and Development (MoFD). It has been tailor developed to meet specific needs of the Government of Somaliland (GoSL) in key business processes, including plan-to-budget, procure-to-pay, revenue-to-cash, and record-to-report. The system is also backed by supporting processes like ICT-to-support delivered by a team of technical and functional support staff and communicate-to-change delivered by a team of change agents.

SLFMIS Infrastructure

The SLFMIS is housed in primary data center located within one of the Ministry of Finance and Development buildings, with a modern disaster recovery center also located with Hargeisa and cloud backup. There is a plan to have regional back up sites. The SLFMIS is accessed by users via the internet which is provided three service providers, one being the primary and the other two being the backup service providers for redundancy.

What is IFMIS?

It is an automated system that enhances efficiency in planning, budgeting, procurement, expenditure management and reporting in the National and County Governments in Somaliland.

IFMIS Funcationality

IFMIS is operational in both the National and County Governments. The system guarantees that each expense is traceable, delivering optimal value to every taxpayer.
